A little bit about me.

Hi, I'm Michelle Burnett. Nice to meet you! I am a freelance UX Designer living in the beautiful mountains of Northern Utah with my family and cat. I have worked many years running a small business where I have worn many hats, from customer care, to human resources and bookkeeping to changing a tire. Whatever is required to get the job done.

I am also a caregiver and autism advocate which gives me an awareness for the differing needs of others which lends to my passion for empathetic design. I have also had a deep appreciation for art, architecture, and antiques. I like combining my love of art with technology to create something that is aesthetically pleasing as well as functional.
